Project Details

Project Motivation

This project aims to uncover the hidden daily rhythms of NYC, analyzing urban mobility, activity zones, and environmental factors to understand the city's "breathing pattern".

Research Questions

  • How do daily rhythms manifest in urban transportation patterns?
  • What are the key factors influencing mobility and movement in NYC?
  • How do noise levels and events correlate with urban activity zones?
  • Can we identify distinct hot and cold zones based on activity patterns?
